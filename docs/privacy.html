Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

SYNC ("we", "our", or "the app") is a couples companion app built by Meherzan Boundless. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use SYNC.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and FaceTime email. These are required to provide core app functionality.

Partner Connection: When you connect with your partner using an invite code, we store the pairing relationship to enable shared features.

Content You Create: This includes mood entries, date night plans, shared photos and videos, game activity, manifestation goals, gentle days settings, and messages exchanged between you and your partner within the app.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your location to share it with your connected partner on the map and to enable location-based features like nearby date night suggestions. Location is only accessed while the app is in use.

Device Information: We collect push notification tokens to deliver notifications. We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features between you and your partner
  • To send push notifications (date reminders, partner activity, SOS alerts)
  • To enable the SOS emergency call feature using your stored phone number and FaceTime email
  • To display your location to your connected partner on the shared map
  • To store and display shared photos, videos, and memories
  • To facilitate games, mood tracking, and other couple activities

3. Data Sharing

Your personal data is shared only with your connected partner within the app. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • MongoDB Atlas - Media storage and additional data services
  • Expo - Push notification delivery and app updates

These services process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore and MongoDB Atlas, both of which use encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is restricted through security rules that ensure only you and your connected partner can access your shared information.

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Firestore security rules that enforce data access boundaries
  • No storage of passwords in plain text

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Correct inaccurate information through your account settings
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for location sharing, notifications, or other permissions through your device settings at any time

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera - To capture moments and share photos with your partner
  • Photo Library - To share photos and save memories
  • Location - To share your location with your partner on the map
  • Contacts - To help you invite your partner to the app
  • Calendar - To schedule date nights and add events
  • Notifications - To send date reminders, partner activity alerts, and SOS notifications
  • Microphone - For voice messages and video calls
  • Face ID - For secure account access

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings. The app will still function with reduced features if permissions are denied.

7. Children's Privacy

SYNC is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
